Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Easy Song

How does Easy Song help me learn languages? +

Easy Song makes language learning engaging by combining music videos with synchronized lyrics, real-time translations, and detailed explanations. You learn vocabulary, grammar, and cultural context naturally while listening to songs you enjoy. The interactive format helps you remember words and phrases better than traditional methods.

What languages are supported? +

Currently, Easy Song focuses on Spanish songs with English translations and explanations. We're actively working on adding more languages including French, Portuguese, Italian, and German. Check back regularly for updates!

Is there a free version? +

Yes! Easy Song is free to download and use. Free users can access all songs in Play Along mode with synchronized lyrics and translations. Premium features include unlimited history, more study mode videos, faster song requests, and an ad-free experience.

What's the difference between Play Along and Study Mode? +

Play Along mode lets you watch music videos with synchronized, scrolling lyrics and toggle translations on/off. It's perfect for casual listening and getting familiar with songs. Study Mode provides deep dives with section-by-section breakdowns, line-by-line explanations of meaning, slang, and cultural context. It's designed for serious learning and understanding nuances.

Can I request specific songs? +

Yes! Premium users can request songs to be added to our library. Premium tier requests are typically completed within 1 week, while Premium Plus requests are completed within 1 day. We're always looking to expand our catalog with popular and educational songs.

Do I need an internet connection? +

Yes, an internet connection is required to stream music videos and load song data. However, once a song is loaded, you can replay it multiple times. Offline mode is a feature we're considering for future Premium Plus updates.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime? +

Absolutely! You can cancel your Premium or Premium Plus subscription at any time through your device's app store settings. You'll continue to have access to premium features until the end of your current billing period, then your account will revert to the free tier.

How accurate are the translations and explanations? +

Our translations and explanations are generated using advanced AI technology and reviewed by language experts. We focus on providing not just literal translations, but also cultural context, slang meanings, and idiomatic expressions. However, language is nuanced, and we always welcome feedback to improve accuracy.
