Privacy Policy

Last updated:

At Easy Song, I take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what information I collect, how I use it, and how I protect it. Since this is a solo project, I keep things simple and transparent.

Information I Collect

When you use Easy Song, I may collect the following information:

  • Account Information: If you create an account, I collect your email address and any profile information you choose to provide (like your name).
  • Usage Data: I collect information about how you use the app, such as which songs you've played, your learning progress, and your preferences.
  • Device Information: Basic device information like your device type and operating system version to help me improve the app.
  • Song History: I store your song history so you can track your learning progress. This is stored securely and is only accessible to you.

How I Use Your Information

I use the information I collect to:

  • Provide and improve the Easy Song service
  • Personalize your learning experience
  • Track your progress and learning history
  • Send you important updates about the app (only if you opt in)
  • Respond to your questions or support requests

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard practices. I use encryption to protect your information and only store what's necessary to provide the service. Your song history and preferences are stored in a secure database and are only accessible through your account.

If you're using the app as a guest (without an account), your data is stored locally on your device and is not sent to my servers.

Third-Party Services

Easy Song uses a few third-party services to function:

  • YouTube: Music videos are streamed from YouTube. I don't control YouTube's privacy practices, so please review their privacy policy.
  • Authentication Services: If you sign up, authentication is handled securely through standard protocols.
  • Analytics: I may use basic analytics to understand how the app is being used and to fix bugs. This data is anonymized.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Export your data (like your song history)
  • Opt out of any marketing communications

To exercise these rights, you can contact me through the app or by email. I'll respond within a reasonable timeframe.

Children's Privacy

Easy Song is not intended for children under 13. I don't knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe I have collected information from a child under 13, please contact me and I'll delete it immediately.

Changes to This Policy

I may update this privacy policy from time to time. If I make significant changes, I'll notify you through the app or by email. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect when changes were made.

Contact Me

If you have questions about this privacy policy or how I handle your data, please contact me through the app or reach out via email. I'm happy to answer any questions.
